Consent Collection Method

We collect express written consent from consumers through the following method(s):

Verbal Consent Script

The following script is read to customers when obtaining consent for SMS messaging:

"Before we proceed, I want to confirm your consent to receive text messages from Creative Revenue Outsourcing at this number.

These messages will be used to send important account notifications and identity verification. Message and data rates may apply.

Please feel free to reply STOP at any time to opt out.

Customer responses are recorded in our customer management system with the following information:

  • Date and time of consent
  • Phone number consented
  • Name of representative who obtained consent
  • Customer's verbal confirmation (Yes/No)

Opt-Out Process

Customers can opt out of receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message
  • Calling our customer care line at (855) 514-3597

Upon receiving an opt-out request, we immediately cease all messaging to that number and send a confirmation message.

Help/Support

Customers can text HELP at any time to receive:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Instructions for opting out
  • Customer support phone number

Message Content

Messages sent from this number include:

  • Customer care responses and follow-ups
  • Service status updates and notifications
  • Account-related communications

We do not send unsolicited marketing messages or share customer information with third parties for messaging purposes.
